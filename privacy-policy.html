Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Roll ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, and profile photos
  • Content: Posts, messages, photos, videos, and other content you create or share
  • Location Data: When you enable location services, we collect your location to show nearby posts, events, and enable location sharing in groups
  • Communications: Messages and communications with other users through our group chat features
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable social features like groups, events, and messaging
  • Show you relevant content based on your location and preferences
  • Send you notifications about events, messages, and app updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information, posts, and location (when shared) are visible to other users based on your privacy settings
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our services (e.g., Firebase for data storage)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

4. Location Data

Location sharing is optional. When you enable location services:

  • We use your location to show nearby posts and events
  • You can share your location with specific groups
  • You can stop sharing your location at any time in the app settings
  • Location data is stored securely and only shared with users you choose

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control location sharing and privacy settings
  • Opt out of certain data collection (though this may limit app functionality)

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Firebase: For authentication, data storage, and analytics (Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Apple Sign In: For authentication (Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/)
  • Google Sign In: For authentication (Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
