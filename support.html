Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?
Tap the "+" button in the Groups tab to create a new group. You can add members and customize your group settings.
How do I share my location with a group?
Open a group, go to the Map tab, and tap "Share Location" to let group members see where you are in real-time.
How do I create an event?
Go to the Activity or Tonight tab, tap the "+" button, and fill in the event details. You can make it public or friends-only.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account from the Profile screen. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the three dots menu on any post or message, then select "Report". You can also email us directly at rolltheapp@gmail.com.
I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?
Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. For other issues, contact us at rolltheapp@gmail.com.
